Hey, Stud!

Click the button below to begin your Weekly Stock Check. Before you begin, please confirm that the items below are complete.

  • You are using the studio laptop, not your personal or the Studio Manager laptop.
  • All unprocessed damages are adjusted with the correct reason code in Brightpearl.
  • Assigned SKUs for the week are organized and in their correct bins.
  • No jewelry is sitting in your DCR, Midmarks, or in the Reprocessing Bins.

Step 1: Brightpearl Inventory Summary

Download a Brightpearl inventory summary.

Here's how:

  1. Login to Brightpearl
  2. Click "Products"
  3. Click "Inventory Summary"
  4. Go to the "warehouse" dropdown menu and select your studio.
  5. Click "Filter".
  6. Click "Export" to download your studio's inventory summary.

The inventory summary will download to your laptop.

Step 2: Converting Brightpearl Inventory Summary into OmniCounts File

Click 'Choose File' and select the Brightpearl Inventory Summary you just downloaded. Then, type in your studio number with no zeros before the number (example: if you are studio 033 CA Fashion Island, just enter 33.) Then click 'Generate'. You can use the OmniCounts generator on the OMNICOUNTS page of this portal.

Open OmniCounts Generator

Step 7: Reconcile your variances.

Once you've closed your count, handoffs@omnicounts.com will send the studio an email with your variance report attached (what Brightpearl says you have versus what you counted today). Download the attachment in that email.

Step 8: Check for major variances

If you see something that looks off, recount that SKU and confirm the quantity matches.

Once you've confirmed all your counts are accurate to what you have on hand, use the variance report to make all necessary adjustments in Brightpearl.
